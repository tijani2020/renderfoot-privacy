Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Renderfoot (“the app”, “we”) is a football lineup and league‑table builder for Android, developed by Abderrahim Tijani. This policy explains what information the app does and does not handle.

In short: Renderfoot has no user accounts, shows no ads, includes no analytics or tracking, and keeps everything you create on your own device.

Information we collect

None. Renderfoot does not ask you to sign in, and it does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information. We operate no servers that store your data and have no way to identify you.

Data stored on your device

Everything you create in Renderfoot is saved locally on your device only:

  • Saved lineups and league tables (team names, formations, the player details you enter, points, and match stats)
  • Any photos you choose to add to a custom player or team
  • Your in‑app preferences

This information never leaves your phone. Uninstalling the app permanently removes it.

Photos

When you build a custom lineup or table, you can optionally add a photo to a player or team. When you do, the app asks permission to access your photo library and uses only the image you pick. That image is stored inside your lineup on your device and is not uploaded anywhere. Renderfoot does not use your camera or microphone.

When you tap Export or Save, the finished lineup or table image is saved to your device’s gallery — only at your request.

Internet connection and third‑party services

Renderfoot connects to the internet only to download public football data — team, squad, and league information and club/player crests — so you can build lineups with real teams. It does this through:

  • Renderfoot’s data service (api.renderfoot.com)
  • API‑Sports media (media.api-sports.io), which hosts club, player, and league images

These requests fetch public information and do not include any personal data about you. As with any internet request, those services may record standard technical details such as your device’s IP address in their own server logs; Renderfoot does not control or receive those logs. All requests are made over a secure (HTTPS) connection.

Renderfoot contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third‑party tracking SDKs.

Children’s privacy

Renderfoot is a general‑audience sports app and is not directed at children under 13. Because we collect no personal information from anyone, we collect none from children.

Your control over your data

  • Delete individual saved lineups or tables inside the app at any time.
  • Uninstall Renderfoot to remove all app data from your device.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above and post the new version at this URL.
